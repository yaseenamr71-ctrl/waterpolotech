The Eggbeater

The eggbeater is a fundamental kicking technique that allows players to maintain vertical position in the water. It involves a circular leg motion where each leg alternates in a scissor-like kick, pushing water downward and outward to create lift. Focus on keeping your knees bent and feet close to the surface for maximum vertical lift. This technique is crucial for treading water during gameplay, enabling quick movements and positioning.
